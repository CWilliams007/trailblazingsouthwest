What makes Trailblazing Southwest different?

Vacation Design

Vacation Design: Making use of my expertise and network of travel outfitters throughout the Southwest region, we’ll design a detailed itinerary proposal that can be further customized to your liking.

What to expect from our itineraries…

  • Flight information
  • Rental car options
  • Accommodation options
  • Route description
  • Destination and national parks information
  • Activities
  • Drive-time/mileage
  • Dining/shopping recommendations

Vacation Booking

Booking: We facilitate the entire booking process for you — flights, lodging and transportation. We’ll take care of everything down to the last detail.

Including:

  • Taking care of any issues pertaining to booking that may arise (flight delays, cancellations, etc.)
  • Procuring upgraded amenities and exclusive bonuses when obtainable
  • Consulting on various travel requirements (passport, visas, travel insurance, etc.)

Vacation Concierge

Concierge: Think of us as your personal travel advocates available on-call — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We do more than plan itineraries and execute bookings. Depending on your desires and personal circumstances, our services may also include:

Including:

  • Providing spontaneous recommendations for unique destinations and activities
  • Booking private or small-group guided tours and excursions (hiking, mountain biking, rock climbing, canyoneering, rafting, paddle boarding, horseback riding, etc.)
  • Making reservations for restaurants, workshops and lectures, museum visits and more!
  • Corresponding with tour operators on your behalf
  • Managing application processes for necessary permits (i.e. backcountry hiking, overnight camping, etc.)
  • Allocating translation services (upon availability) for speakers of foreign languages
  • Assisting with unexpected travel emergencies
