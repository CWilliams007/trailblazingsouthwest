Canyoneering

Canyoneering is the intimate exploration of forgotten Earth. Imagine extreme hiking with a harness, a helmet and appropriate rope systems. Think reverse climbing and scrambling with a little rear-end sliding. Maybe even a lot of rear- end sliding.

Discover Canyoneering

The Southwest houses some of the most amazing canyoneering in the world. Mainly — in Utah and Arizona, with Zion National Park being what many might call the mecca for canyoneering in the Southwest.

Experience granite slots, pine forest pools, trickling streams, rushing waterfalls, sculpted rocks and steep cliffs. Our vendors hold permits to some of the most epic canyons in the Southwest. Providing once-in-a-lifetime adventures for you and your travel companions.

“WE GO DOWN CANYONS. FIND OUT WHAT IS THERE. AND HAVE FABULOUS ADVENTURES.” — Canyoneering USA
